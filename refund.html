Cancellations & Refund Policy

Recharges

All recharges made to your account wallet are final and non-refundable. Once funds are added to your wallet, they cannot be withdrawn or refunded back to your bank account, except through the platform's official withdrawal process (if applicable).

Investments

All investments made into plans are final. An investment cannot be cancelled once it has been activated. The principal amount is locked for the duration of the plan and will be returned according to the terms of that specific plan.

Failed Payments

In the event that a recharge payment is debited from your bank account but does not reflect in your website wallet within 2 hours, please contact our customer support immediately. Provide your transaction ID and a screenshot of the payment. We will verify the transaction with our payment gateway and credit the amount to your wallet if the payment was successful.
